Visualising World Crime Rate

Visualizing the crime situation in different countries around the world is a topic of our interest

The visualisation domain revolves around intentional homicide rates across different countries. The data is pivotal for policymakers, law enforcement agencies, and researchers who aim to understand the reasons behind crime trends and to strategize effective countermeasures.

The visualization is structured in a single column layout, with graphics displayed from top to bottom. This design allows users to scroll through the data easily and comfortably. And this design ensures users can first focus on the data and then interact using the slider. In most visualizations, Countries were listed on the X-axis and the rate on the Y-axis for intuitive understanding.

Map of crime rates in major countries in the world

The use of visualization in map format is very important, especially for the visualization scenarios of the current world crime situation. It allows users to intuitively view the world map and also see the crime rate statistics of each country.We can clearly see that the crime rate situation in Central America and Africa is relatively serious
